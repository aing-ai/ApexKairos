Support the Mission

This is a solo build. One person, limited resources, real results — 12 years of architecture condensed into live software. Your support directly funds development of airig.ai and ApexKairos — open cooperative systems built for everyone, owned by no one.

Why does this need funding?

airig.ai is live. ApexKairos is being built. Both are pre-revenue — the cooperative economics model means users earn when others save, and the vault only fills when the network grows. Until then, development costs come from the founder directly.

Server costs, API access, domain fees, hardware maintenance, and the time required to build something the world hasn't seen yet — that's what your donation covers. Not marketing. Not salaries. The actual build.

30% of all commercial license earnings are distributed back to the community through the AING/airig.ai cooperative pool — the same system this project is built on.
