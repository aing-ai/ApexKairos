This is a solo build. One person, limited resources, real results — 12 years of architecture condensed into live software. Your support directly funds airig.ai and ApexKairos — open cooperative systems built for everyone, owned by no one.

Why does this need funding?

airig.ai is live. ApexKairos is being built. Both are pre-revenue — the cooperative economics model means the vault only fills when the network grows. Until then, development costs come from the founder directly.

Your donation covers: server costs, API access, domain fees, hardware maintenance, and the time required to build something the world hasn't seen yet. Not marketing. The actual build.

30% of all commercial license earnings are distributed back to the community through the AING/airig.ai cooperative pool.
